About the Project

Escojo Enseñar — "I Choose to Teach" — was produced and directed during my Peace Corps service in the Dominican Republic (2010–2012). The series supports new and practicing teachers with foundational pedagogy and practical classroom strategies, delivered in Spanish and designed for the local educational context.

As both producer and director, I led the series end-to-end: identifying training needs, scripting, casting, shooting, and editing video content organized into foundational and practical instructional units.
